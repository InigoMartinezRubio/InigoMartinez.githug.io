First of all. Hello reader!

I'm Iñigo Martínez, a passionate gamer and developer. I've loved videogames and everything that comes with them since as far as I can remember. I still feel the emotion of grabbing my new GameBoy and starting F-Zero for the first time. It blew me away! And that is exactly what I want to transmit to players that play the games I work on. A experience they will never forget.

Following with the skills I have acquired. I've learned to 3D Model in Maya, 3DS Max and Blender. I also know how to texture and create materials in both Substance Painter and Designer. And in the case of game engines. I have deep understanding and knowledge in Unity (All of the pipelines) and Unreal Engine 4/5. Knowing coding languages such as C# and C++.

My studies consits in a degree in Game and Narrative Design in Universidad Francisco de Vitoria.

Thank you very much for reading this. I hope we can work together in the future.

Íñigo.
